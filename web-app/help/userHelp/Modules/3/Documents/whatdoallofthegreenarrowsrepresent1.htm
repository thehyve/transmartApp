What do all of the green arrows represent? 

Clicking a green arrow pointing upward and to the right after a gene name opens a window containing links to the following external sites:

■    Entrez Gene (NCBI)

■    Entrez Global (NCBI)

■    Pictor (Johnson & Johnson)

■    Hydra (Johnson & Johnson)

■    Gene Cards (Xennex)

■    TargetCV (Johnson & Johnson)

■    Google Scholar
