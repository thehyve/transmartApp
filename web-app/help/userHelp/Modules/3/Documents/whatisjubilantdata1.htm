What is Jubilant data? 

The Literature tab presents the user with information about the search terms that have been found in published literature. Jubilant is the name of a company that was sourced to curate thousands of peer-reviewed journal articles in the fields of oncology and immunology. Each article was reviewed and categorized into a 2009 MeSH (Medical Subject Headings via the National Library of Medicine) ontology. Jubilant also extracted mechanisms of action from most of the research articles so that each article can be explored in Ariadne's Pathway Studio software – exposing the underlying biochemistry.
