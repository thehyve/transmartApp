Hazard Ratio and Relative Risk

The two groups being compared in a survival analysis are sometimes thought of as the treatment group and the control group.  The hazard ratio and the relative risk ratio calculated in a survival analysis are ratios of the treatment group over the control group.

In a Dataset Explorer survival analysis, these ratios are based on Subset 2 results over Subset 1 results.
