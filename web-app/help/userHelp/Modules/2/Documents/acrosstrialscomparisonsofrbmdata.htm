Across-Trials Comparisons of RBM Data

To perform an across-trials comparison of RBM data:

1.  Run tranSMART, then click the Dataset Explorer tab.

2.  In the left pane, click the new Across Trials tab:

3.  Open the Across Trials node, then open the RBM node.

4.  Open the Baseline node, then open Observed or Z Score. 

5.  Open an antigen node.

6.  Drag the studies to use in the comparison into the same subset box in Subset 1.

In the following example, three studies have been dragged into Subset 1 from the node representing the antigen Adiponectin:

 

7.  Open the Primary Endpoint node, then open Observed or Z Score. 

8.  Open the same antigen node.

9.  Drag the studies to use in the comparison into the same subset box in Subset 2.

In the following example, the same three studies have been dragged into Subset 2:

10. Click Generate Summary Statistics.

The comparison appears at the bottom of the Results/Analysis view.
