Export ResNet

Click the Export ResNet button to generate an Ariadne Genomics’ Pathway Studio visualization of the interactions between the biomarkers returned from a literature search. You can do so either by opening Pathway Studio and importing the literature data now, or by saving the information to a file that can be imported into Pathway Studio later.

When you click Export ResNet, tranSMART exports any alterations, inhibitors, and interactions results in the oncology dataset, and any alterations and interactions results in the asthma dataset, to Pathway Studio.  Export to Pathway Studio of asthma protein effects  is not supported.

For an example of importing curated literature data into Pathway Studio, see the section Create a Visualization of a Literature Search Result in the tranSMART JnJ Training: Advanced guide.
