ResNet Tab

When you click the ResNet tab after a search against one or more genes, pathways, and/or diseases, tranSMART displays controls for performing an Ariadne Genomics Medscan search. These controls are displayed in the tranSMART results pane.

Note:  If a security warning appears when you click this tab, see Security Warning When Selecting Pictor, ResNet, or GeneGO.

To begin the Medscan search, click Search.  Optionally, you can modify the search parameters before you click Search.

The first time you click the ResNet tab in a tranSMART session, you are prompted for your PWS user ID and password.
