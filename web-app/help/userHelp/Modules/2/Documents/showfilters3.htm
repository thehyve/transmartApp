Show Filters

The Show Filters button appears at the top of the results list. Click this button to further refine the search results.

When you click the button, a window containing filter fields appears (shown below), and the Show Filters button is replaced by the Hide Filters button.

In the figure below, filter selections are set for the broadest possible search.

To narrow the search:

1.  Deselect one or more check boxes in the Repository and Document Type sections.

For example, if you are looking for documents related to conferences, deselect the Biomarker, DIP, and Jubilant Oncology boxes.

2.  Click Filter Results to start the search.
