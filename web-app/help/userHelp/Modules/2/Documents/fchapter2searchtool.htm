FChapter 2:  Search Tool

A tranSMART search returns information in one or more of the following result categories:

■    Clinical Trials – Internal Johnson & Johnson clinical trials.

■    mRNA Analysis – Public or internal mRNA experiments.

■    mRNA Profiles – Gene expression profiles from public cancer datasets curated by the Dana Farber Cancer Institute GCOD database.

■    Literature – Curated documents within broad subject matter categories such as oncology and asthma.

■    Documents – Documents from Johnson & Johnson’s document repositories.

■    Pictor – Johnson & Johnson’s interface to pathway databases such as KEGG, GeneGO, and Ingenuity.

■    ResNet – Ariadne Genomics functionality for performing search and pathway operations.

■    GeneGo – Direct access to the EZ Search functionality of GeneGo’s MetaCore.
