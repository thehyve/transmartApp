New Parameters for Generating Gene Expression Heat Maps

When you generate a heat map for a mRNA platform, you can now further refine the contents of the heat map by selecting datasets from one or more of the study’s GPL platforms and tissue types.

You can select these and other gene expression parameters on the Compare Subsets-Pathway Selection dialog:

Optionally, you can vary the values of the parameters on this dialog while keeping the definition of the cohorts in the subset definition boxes constant.
