Viewing a Cross-Trial Analysis

A cross-trial analysis allows you to view data and visualizations for multiple analyses. You can include analyses from the same study and/or across studies.

The following figure shows a cross-trial analysis consisting of three analyses:

 

The contents of the top part of a cross-trial analysis display varies, depending on whether you click the Summary Table (the default), Gene Charts, or Heatmaps button. The bottom part of the view, labeled Selected Analyses, always contains the list of selected analyses and, when you click an analysis name, a heatmap of the analysis.
