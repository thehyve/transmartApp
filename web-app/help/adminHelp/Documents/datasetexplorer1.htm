Dataset Explorer

Dataset Explorer lets you compare data generated for test subjects in two different sub-groups, called subsets, of the overall study population, based on criteria and points of comparison that you specify.  Dataset Explorer is useful to help you test a hypothesis that involves the criteria and points of comparison you select for the comparison.
